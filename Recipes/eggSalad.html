Egg Salad

Description

Egg Salad - Made of Mashed Egg mixed with a sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 Boiled Eggs
  • Mustard and Mayonnaise Sauce
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Boil the Eggs and mash them
  2. Mix 1 cup of mayonnaise with 1 teaspoon of mustard and mix it with the mashed eggs.
  3. Place on a toast and place tomato slices on top to serve.
